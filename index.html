About

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the laboratory of David Liu at Harvard University and the Broad Institute.

In my graduate studies I discovered that redesigning enzymes with AI can elevate their evolutionary potential, enabling the evolution of AI-redesigned proteases with enhanced properties that are inaccessible to their natural counterparts.

With my colleagues in the Liu lab, we applied this discovery to create next-generation base and prime editors for therapeutic gene editing.
